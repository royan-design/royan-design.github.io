This article introduces the parameter setting of laser marking machine.

General parameters

Blackening parameters

The stainless steel blackening parameters are adjusted as follows

(1) Power setting: 75% - 85%
(2) Speed setting: 100-150
(3) Frequency setting: 20
(4) Fill line spacing: 0.05
(5) Focus upward deviation: 3-4mm
